Kismet Foundation

Feeding the Future of South Africa

At Kismet, our passion for meat and culinary excellence is matched only by our dedication to the communities we call home. The Kismet Foundation ensures that the prosperity of our restaurants directly supports those facing food insecurity across Gauteng.

We believe that every meal served in our restaurants should contribute to a meal for someone in need. Through our "Seed-to-Sizzle" program, we provide local farmers with the technology and resources needed to scale their operations ethically.

Kitchen Incubator

We provide professional workspace and mentorship for aspiring local chefs and street-food entrepreneurs.

Community Pantry

Direct support to local food banks and NGOs, providing high-quality protein to under-resourced neighborhoods.

Sustainable Sourcing

Investing in ethical livestock farming practices and training local farmers in regenerative agriculture.
